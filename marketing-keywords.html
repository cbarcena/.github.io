Keywords

Ecommerce Tips See also Ecommerce

Keyword reference is the most important in ecommerce SEO strategies. Below we go over proper methodologies to consider when compiling your list of keywords for your website.

First, unite clear and unique page titles and descriptions for each product page, and don’t reuse them. For instance, if you’re a drop shipper and using manufacturer product descriptions on your product page use Canonical URLS.

A canonical url is a tag that tells a search engine where the page content originally came from, within the head of the web doc
Example: <link rel=”canonical” href="http://www.____.com”>

Getting the most out of keyword research

When trying to get the most out of your keyword research keep in mind to get the results you're looking for choose quality over quantity. Here's how:

  • Crafted Naturally: Use Keywords within site content as if you’re talking
  • Stuffing text with keywords can give the impression the site is spam
  • Cloaked text can be prevented from searches
  • Keyword List and Planning
    • Make a list of everything a buyer may enter
    • Brainstorm an actual list
    • Use Google’s Built in Suggestion list
      While researching, use the drop down list of
      suggested content to get an idea of what will display.

  • Use Google Adwords
    • Use Google’s Keyword Planner tool
    • Explore Competitors for each keyword
    • Finalize your list, consider using excel
    • Click tab “Keyword Ideas” and add to list

Carrying out competitor research

  • Perform search for each keyword and make note of any major players in that field
  • Use “SEM Rush” to identify top ranking sites for a term
  • Run a side by side comparison of your top competitors, such as
    “Side By Side SEO Comparison”
  • Differentiate yourself as much as possible
  • Aim to be the big fish in a small pond

A good example of a website using proper keyword usage and placement can be found at Dodocase.comCheck out the tag line, it includes links as well as keywords. The H1 tag uses headers, the keywords are internal links and the writing is done naturally for maximum usability and comprehension. This is a very good example of proper use of keywords within a website.
