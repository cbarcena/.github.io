How can Social Media Help?

Social media has many benefits and learning how to take advantage of them is essential. Below is a brief listing of what you can learn by networking with those within the industry.

Land a job

  • Referrals - best way
  • Recruiters - play bigger role in industry
  • Attend meetups, social events and conferences
  • Get to know others that share similar interests

Social learning opportunities

  • Learn where and what to read and look up answers
  • Learn to interact with other developers
  • Keep up with the industry
  • Team sport
  • Make contacts 30% get jobs through referrals, less than 20% online
  • Attend Meetups www.meetups.com
  • Local companies, hungry for developers www.Barcamp.org
