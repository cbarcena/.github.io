Strategies

Most people don’t buy products just to spend money; they’re looking for solutions to a problem. Before wasting time and effort on SEO or marketing efforts it’s important to ask yourself three questions.

  • Who are your customers?
    This will help to define your audience right off the bat.

  • What problem do they have?
    Look at it from a customers’ point of view.

  • How can you address their problems?
    Create personas with detailed representations of your users/customers.
    Personas should include jobs, daily routine, experience, etc.

    • Personas affects the messaging on your website, so be sure to choose a persona before picking a theme/design. Along with getting to know your customers be sure to consider their knowledge of your product as well.


    Organizing your products

    There are several ways to entice your potential buyers by using strategies such as up sale methods in addition to special offers, such examples include:

    • Bundles: educational, inclusive, offers a price break
    • Add-ons: sell products that enhance others, save catalog space, easy up sell
    • Configurable Products: offer a single product with customizable options
    • Subscriptions: convenience is a feature, can reward customer

    Packaging and your message around your products is incredibly important, do so by offering pre-sales to validate your product. Also keep in mind that creating a site takes a lot of effort, before committing, try selling just one product. Get your product in front of the people you want to sell to, and get an idea of their need of the product. If there’s not enough interest, you can always cancel the order. So get feedback, testimonials and get form validation from users.


    Marketing Your Site

    Using marketing to increase site traffic will get you quick results, and has many options available on the web. To name a couple of the more popular services available are Google AdWords and Facebook Advertising. These are paid services, however both provide promising results.

    Consider content marketing to better educate your buyers on the products your selling and always consider Cost of Acquistion (COA)
    see CLV.


    Funding assistance is available for those seeking investment options, such websites include:

    • www.kickstarter.com
    • www.crowdfunding.com

    Lastly, keep in mind that No Effort = No Traffic.
    It's imperative to put every effort forward in reaching out to those potential buyers and completely researching the industry you're planning to enter.
