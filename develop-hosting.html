Hosting Options

Looking at web hosting plans I’ve heard some really good things about Doster.com and GoDaddy. Doster.com I haven’t had a chance to try out plans start at $3.75 a month, as for GoDaddy plans start at $3.99 a month. I use to volunteer at a place here in town just about a year ago, there website was hosted by Godaddy and I found it to be fairly easy to navigate. They had optimum customer service and questions get answered quickly and accurately. As for Doster.com, a close friend of mine has been using their services for about 12 years now and has no complaints. They offer a lot of free one click installs, great customer services and server up-time. If looking for the free route, several years back I registered a couple web domains with 000webhost.com but have yet to take it for a spin. Another popular one that I’ve heard of but have yet to try out would be weebly.com. Had it not been for this class I wouldn’t know what to look for in searching for the right server and what to look for, I am so thankful for that and I’m hoping to tie the loose ends that I needed so much to complete any pending projects I’ve had sitting on the back burner
