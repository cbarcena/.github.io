What is SEO?

  • Search Engine Optimization
  • The process of accessing current on and off page factors of a website
  • Improving these factors to boost rankings in a search engine like Google

Understanding on-page vs off-page SEO

  • On Page: Keywords you’re targeting, written content, headings, titles, etc. Page URLS and the way these are structured, Internal links such as footers as navigation
  • Off page: links to your website from other websites
    • Social Media
    • Reputable industry sites ideally from large companies that currently rank well in a similar
    • Blog Posts
    • Product review sites

The Importance of Usability

  • Acquisition SEO with Retention Usability equals
  • Lower Bounce Rates
  • Higher Click Through
  • Site Speed
    • Ensure site is easy to use to help improve site
    • The more you encourage site visitors to explore your site and share your links the better your rankings will become
  • Focus on usability
    • Design and write for customers first then search engines
    • Never sacrifice usability for rankings
    • Give visitors direct access to information - don’t bury it
    • Focus on proper design techniques

Setting expectations and establishing realistic goals for your SEO results

  • Expectations for SEO Results
    • Results may shift up and down on a weekly basis
    • Its an ongoing process, results not immediate
    • Ensure your site deserves to be top spot
    • Target Specific Searches
    • Pay your way to the top - “fake it til you make it”

Exploring how SEO affects the buying process

  • Need Recognition - Target more buyers early on in purchase for a higher number for viewers, blog posts, most number of buyers
  • Solution Exploration - Search Engine Optimization
  • Evaluate Options - target competitor keywords
  • Buy
  • Evaluation - product reviews, post purchase evaluations, less buyers

Optimizing your product pages

Product Page Strategies

  • Use “breadcrumbs” to simplify product navigation
    Breadcrumbs: a link that shows a user where they are on the site
  • Add social sharing buttons to encourage link building
  • Use consistent page titles
    Ex: product name - product category | business name
  • Write unique product descriptions, do not copy manufacturer descriptions
